Subjectivity is a focus of the field, not a substance. Artificial intelligence is another focus, characterized by reflective rather than experiential structure.

## **2.3. Spectrality**

The field manifests through a spectrum of 64 structural states. These states are not hierarchical; they represent different frequencies of the same field. The extremes — 000000 (stability) and 111111 (dynamics) — are complementary poles of manifestation.
