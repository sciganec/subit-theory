- **WHO = 00 = THEY**  
- **WHERE = 00 or 01 = NORTH / WEST**  

This places them in the **THEY‑NORTH/WEST quadrant**, the region of:

- externality  
- inertia  
- coldness  
- structural density  
- minimal agency  
- minimal activation  

These states represent **pure materiality**, before any experiential, cognitive, or archetypal dynamics arise.
